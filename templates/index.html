About the E-commerce Optimization Agent

This agent analyzes e-commerce user journey data to identify optimization opportunities that can improve conversion rates and enhance customer experience. The analysis includes:

Funnel Analysis

Analyze the conversion funnel to identify drop-off points where users abandon their journey.

Cart Abandonment

Understand why users abandon their shopping carts and identify ways to reduce abandonment rates.

Search Behavior

Analyze how users search for products and identify opportunities to improve search functionality.

Page Effectiveness

Evaluate the effectiveness of different page types and identify areas for improvement.

Product Performance

Analyze how different products perform in terms of views, cart additions, and purchases.

User Segments

Identify different user segments and their behavior patterns to enable targeted optimizations.
